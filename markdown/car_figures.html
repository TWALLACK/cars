Popular cars in Massachusetts

2018-09-25

There were 4,571,544 private passenger cars and light-trucks registered in Massachusetts in 2018, according to data from the state Registry of Motor Vehicles.

We used the data for a (http://apps.bostonglobe.com/metro/graphics/2018/09/cars-by-town/ “Boston Globe story.”) Billy wrote the story. I analyzed the data. And Patrick Garvin created the graphics on the Boston Globe site.
